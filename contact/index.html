Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

Yes, we work by appointment to ensure each pet receives our full attention. Walk-ins are welcome if we have availability, but booking ahead is recommended.

How long does grooming take?

Most grooming sessions take 1-3 hours depending on your pet's size, coat condition, and the services requested. We'll provide an estimate when you book.
